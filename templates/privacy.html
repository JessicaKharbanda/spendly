Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you add, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform, but these providers do not have access to your individual expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, or if you would like to request deletion of your account and data, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
